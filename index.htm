David Eden

Senior UX Designer

Welcome

I am a User Experience Designer with over 15 years of experience in interaction design, visual design, and user research. I have applied my user-centered design methodology and my prototyping skills to designing websites, desktop and web applications, mobile and touch-enabled devices, and hardware products. My skills have helped bridge the gaps between product owners, software development teams, and the end users by providing design leadership and forming consensus throughout the product development lifecycle. This has proven to be invaluable in Agile development by promoting Design Thinking to understand user goals and pain points, and further, by helping software development teams fully understand the intent of product design through high-fidelity prototypes. I would like to contribute to creating the best products for our customers who will ultimately decide our success.
